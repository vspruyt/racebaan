The Real Stig

Race with your friends

The Real Stig is an arcade racing game you can play directly in your browser. Create a room, invite your friends, chase clean laps, and compete for faster times on the all-time leaderboard.

  • Fast arcade handling built for quick rematches
  • Private room IDs and shareable links for multiplayer races
  • All-time lap leaderboard for bragging rights
  • Playable on desktop and mobile web browsers

How to play

Pick a display name, enter or generate a room ID, and start the race. Share the room link with a friend to join the same match, then push for the fastest lap and a spot on the leaderboard.

Why players find it fun

The Real Stig focuses on short sessions, readable controls, and close multiplayer races. It is built for players who want instant competition without installs, accounts, or heavy setup.

Frequently asked questions

  • Do I need to install anything? No, it runs in the browser.
  • Can I play with friends? Yes, create a room and share the link.
  • Is there a leaderboard? Yes, the homepage shows all-time best laps.
  • Does it work on phones? Yes, the game supports mobile browsers.
